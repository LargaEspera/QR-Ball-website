The "QR Ball" game.

This is a game about a ball that is located in labyrinths like a QR code. That's why it has such a name. Here you have to go through the labyrinths. At each level, you have a limited time to complete it. Besides, the game gets harder with each subsequent level. At the moment there are 5 levels available in the game. The game is under development, so if you have a desire to support the project, below will be a post on how to do it (donation alerts), It is also worth adding that the game is being developed by one person on the UNITY engine. The author of the game is Diyar Muhidenov, a first-year student at Astana IT University.


If you want, you can support the further development of the game by following the link to donationalegts. Fundraising is voluntary, thank you!
